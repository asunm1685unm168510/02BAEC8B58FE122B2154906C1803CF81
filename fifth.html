Vada Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram) soaked for 4-5 hours
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Drain the soaked urad dal and grind it to a smooth batter in a blender or food processor. Add a little water if necessary.
  2. Transfer the batter to a mixing bowl and add the chopped onions, green chilies, grated ginger, asafoetida, cumin seeds, black pepper (optional), and salt. Mix well.
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  4. Wet your hands and take a small portion of the batter. Shape it into a small ball and make a hole in the center (like a doughnut).
  5. Carefully drop the shaped vada into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy on both sides. Do not overcrowd the pan.
  6. Remove the vadas and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
  7. Serve hot with chutney or sambar.

Enjoy your delicious Vadas!
