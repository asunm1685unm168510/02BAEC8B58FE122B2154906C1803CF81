Kamba Koozh Recipe

Ingredients
  • 1 cup Kambu (Pearl Millet)
  • 1/4 cup Rice
  • 1/2 cup Buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup grated Coconut
  • 1 small Green Chili, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp Ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1/4 tsp Asafoetida (Hing)
  • 1/4 tsp Salt (or to taste)
  • Water as required
Instructions
  • 1. Soak the Kambu (Pearl Millet) and rice for 4-5 hours or overnight.
  • 2. Drain the water and grind the soaked ingredients into a smooth batter, adding little water as required.
  • 3. In a pan, heat some water and bring it to a boil. Add the ground batter slowly and stir continuously to avoid lumps.
  • 4. Once the batter starts to thicken and cook, add grated coconut, ginger, and green chili. Stir well.
  • 5. Cook for 5-7 minutes, then add the buttermilk and stir. Add salt and asafoetida (hing) for flavor.
  • 6. Continue cooking for another 3-4 minutes until the koozh is fully thickened and reaches a porridge-like consistency.
  • 7. Serve hot, optionally garnished with a little extra grated coconut or fresh coriander.
Note: Kamba Koozh is a refreshing dish often enjoyed during summer, offering a cooling effect and great for hydration.
