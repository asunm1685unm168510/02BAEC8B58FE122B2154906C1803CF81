Masala Dosa Recipe

About Masala Dosa

Masala Dosa is a popular South Indian dish made of a crispy rice and lentil crepe filled with a spiced potato mixture. It is served with chutneys and sambar.

Ingredients

  • For the dosa batter:
    • 1 cup rice
    • 1/4 cup urad dal (split black gram)
    • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
    • Water as needed
    • Salt to taste
  • For the potato filling:
    • 3 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
    • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
    • 2 tablespoons oil
    • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
    • Salt to taste
  • For the chutneys:
    • Coconut chutney
    • Tamarind chutney

Instructions

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds in water for about 4-6 hours or overnight.
  2. Grind the soaked ingredients into a smooth batter using a little water. The batter should be thick but pourable.
  3. Allow the batter to ferment for 8-12 hours in a warm place.
  4. For the filling: Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, and let them splutter.
  5. Add onions, green chilies, and ginger. Saut√© until the onions are soft.
  6. Add turmeric powder, mashed potatoes, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  7. To cook the dosa: Heat a non-stick pan or tawa. Pour a ladleful of dosa batter and spread it thin in a circular motion.
  8. Cook until the edges turn golden and crispy. Place a spoonful of the potato filling on one side and fold the dosa over it.
  9. Serve hot with coconut chutney and tamarind chutney.
