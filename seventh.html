How to Make Puttu

A Traditional Kerala Breakfast Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup water (adjust as needed)
  • Banana leaves or Puttu maker (if available)
  • Optional: Sugar, ghee, or coconut milk for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, add the rice flour, grated coconut, and salt. Mix them well.
  2. Gradually add water to the mixture, stirring continuously to make a crumbly dough. The dough should be moist but not too sticky.
  3. If you have a Puttu maker, fill the bottom chamber with water and bring it to a boil. If you're using banana leaves, cut them into pieces to form small cylinders.
  4. Layer the Puttu maker with coconut and the dough mixture. Keep alternating layers of dough and coconut until the container is full.
  5. Steam the Puttu for about 10-15 minutes until itâ€™s fully cooked.
  6. Remove from the steamer, and serve hot with sugar, ghee, or coconut milk, if desired.
