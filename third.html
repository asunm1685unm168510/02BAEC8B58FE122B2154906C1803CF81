Idly Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Rice
  • 1/4 cup Urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 tsp Fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)

Instructions

1. Wash the rice and urad dal separately in plenty of water. Soak them for about 4-6 hours.
2. After soaking, drain the water. Blend the rice and dal (separately) to a smooth batter using a little water as needed.
3. Mix the rice and dal batter together in a large bowl. Add salt and mix well.
4. Cover the bowl and let the batter ferment for 12-24 hours, depending on the climate.
5. Once the batter has fermented and become fluffy, itâ€™s ready to make idlies.
6. Grease the idly molds with a little oil and pour the batter into the molds.
7. Steam the idlies in a steamer or pressure cooker (without the weight) for about 10-15 minutes or until they are cooked through.
8. Remove the idlies from the molds and serve hot with chutney and sambar.
